Amount Ingredients
2 cups Fresh strawberries
2 cups Milk
1 cup Heavy cream
1/2 cup Sour cream
2 tbs White sugar

  1. Puree strawberries, milk, cream and sour cream in a blender or food processor until smooth.
  2. Stir in sugar to taste.
  3. Chill 8 hours or overnight in refrigerator before serving.
